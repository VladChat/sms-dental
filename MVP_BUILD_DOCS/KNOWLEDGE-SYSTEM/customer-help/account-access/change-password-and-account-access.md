# Change password and account access

## Summary

As a clinic owner, you sign in to your **account** to manage your business
profile, phone number, SMS approval, and billing. You can change your password from
inside your account, or reset it from the sign-in page if you forget it. If you are
ever locked out, contact support.

## Applies to

Clinic owners who manage the account. (Front-desk staff use the separate
**workspace** — see below.)

## What this means

- **Owners use the account.** Your account is where you manage setup and settings
  for your clinic.
- **Front desk uses the workspace.** Front-desk staff have a separate area
  (the workspace) for handling patient replies, and do not use the owner account.
  See [Front-desk workspace overview](../workspace/front-desk-workspace-overview.md).

### Signing in

You sign in with your email and password on the sign-in page. After signing in,
you land in your account.

### Resetting your password (forgot password)

If you forget your password:

1. On the sign-in page, choose **Forgot password?**
2. Enter your account email.
3. You will receive a password reset link by email.
4. Open the link and set a new password.

For your security, the reset link is sent to your account email, and the system
gives the same confirmation message whether or not an account exists for that
email.

### Changing your password while signed in

If you are already signed in, you can change your password from your account under
**Account access → Change password**. You enter your current password, then your
new password, and confirm it. A valid password must be at least 8 characters and
include at least one letter and one number.

## What you can do

- Sign in to your account to manage your clinic.
- Change your password any time from **Account access** while signed in.
- Reset your password from the sign-in page if you forget it.
- Keep your password private — never share it, and support will never ask you to
  send it.

## What to expect

- A reset link arrives at your account email; open it to set a new password.
- Changing your password while signed in takes effect right away and does not
  affect your other settings.
- Adding front-desk team members through the account is a future capability and is
  not connected yet — for now, contact support if you need help with team access.

## When to contact support

Contact support if:

- you cannot sign in or are locked out,
- you did not receive a password reset email (check spam first), or
- you need help with team or account access.

Email: **support@missedcallsdental.com**
