# Remove a number

## Summary

**Remove number** stops a business number from working and schedules it to be
permanently removed later. It stops calls and texting for that number right away,
and billing changes apply to your next cycle. For a limited time before permanent
removal, you may still be able to restore it.

## Applies to

Clinic owners who want to stop using a business number on their account.

## What this means

When you choose **Remove number** for a business number:

- **It stops working immediately.** Calls and texting routing for that number stop
  right away — the number is no longer in service for your clinic.
- **It is scheduled for permanent removal.** Removing it now does not necessarily
  finish removing it from the underlying phone system at that exact moment; the
  permanent removal completes later.
- **It stays visible and restorable for a limited time.** Until permanent removal
  is complete, the number can usually still be restored. See
  [Restore a removed number](restore-a-removed-number.md).

The action is called **Remove number** — that is the customer action for stopping
service on a number.

## What you can do

- Open the **Phone number** section of your account, find the number, and choose
  **Remove number**.
- Confirm the removal when prompted. Removal takes effect immediately for service.
- If you change your mind soon after, check whether the number is still
  restorable.

## What to expect

- **Service stops immediately** for the removed number.
- **Billing changes apply next cycle.** Removing a number does not create an
  instant refund or credit for the current period. The number may still appear in
  your current-cycle charges but is excluded from your next-cycle estimate. See
  [Current vs next billing cycle](../billing/current-vs-next-cycle.md).
- **Restore is time-limited.** You may be able to restore the number until
  permanent removal completes, but we do **not** promise a fixed window (such as a
  set number of days). Once permanent removal is complete, the number cannot be
  restored and you would need to add a new number.
- Removing one number never affects your other numbers — your remaining numbers
  keep working.

## When to contact support

Contact support if:

- you removed a number by mistake and can no longer restore it,
- a removed number still seems active, or
- you are unsure whether removing a number will affect your texting or billing.

Email: **support@missedcallsdental.com**
