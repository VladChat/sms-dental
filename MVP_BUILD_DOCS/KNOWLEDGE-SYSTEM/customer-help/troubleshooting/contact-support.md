# Contact support

## Summary

If something is not working or you have a question this help center does not
answer, contact support at **support@missedcallsdental.com**. Including a few clear
details helps us help you faster — and there are a few things you should never send
for your safety.

## Applies to

Clinic owners and front-desk staff who need help.

## What this means

Support is here for setup questions, account and access issues, billing questions,
SMS approval and texting questions, and anything that looks like it is not behaving
the way you expect.

Many common situations are covered in this help center first — for example, why
texting may not be active yet, or how to reset your password. It is worth a quick
look before reaching out:

- [Why SMS is not active immediately](../sms-approval/why-sms-is-not-active-immediately.md)
- [Change password and account access](../account-access/change-password-and-account-access.md)

## What you can do

When you contact support, include enough detail for us to find and understand the
issue:

- your **clinic name**,
- your **account email**,
- the **business number** involved (if the issue is about a number or texting),
- the **page or area** you were on (for example, account billing, or the
  workspace),
- **what you expected** to happen, and
- **what actually happened** (a short description, and the wording of any message
  you saw).

### Please do NOT send

For your privacy and security, never include:

- **passwords** — support will never ask for your password,
- **full payment card details**,
- **patient medical details** or anything about a patient's care, or
- other private information that is not needed to describe the issue.

If you need to reference a patient, a phone number is enough — do not include
medical information.

## What to expect

- Send your message to **support@missedcallsdental.com** with the details above.
- For some issues, support may need to review your account's status (for example,
  your texting or billing status) to help resolve it.
- Clear details up front usually mean a faster resolution.

## When to contact support

Reach out when:

- you are locked out or cannot access your account,
- texting or a number is not working the way you expect and the help articles did
  not resolve it,
- a charge or billing detail is unclear, or
- anything else looks wrong.

Email: **support@missedcallsdental.com**
