# What SMS approval means

## Summary

SMS approval is the carrier approval process that business texting needs before
it can be activated. It confirms that your clinic is a real business sending
appropriate, expected messages. Once approved and configured, your texting
service can send missed-call follow-ups to patients.

## Applies to

Clinic owners completing setup or wondering what the **SMS Approval Information**
step in their account is for.

## What this means

Mobile carriers require businesses to be approved before they send text messages
to customers. SMS approval is that process, in plain terms. It exists to keep
business texting trustworthy and to keep legitimate messages from being blocked
or filtered as spam.

For your clinic, SMS approval confirms a few simple things:

- you are a real, identifiable business,
- your texts are an expected follow-up to patients who contacted you, and
- you handle opt-outs properly (patients can reply STOP to stop receiving texts).

The kind of texting your clinic uses — a single, professional follow-up after a
missed call — is exactly the appropriate, low-volume, expected use that approval
is designed for. Your messages are not marketing blasts.

> SMS approval is the carrier approval process required before some business
> texting can be activated. (The technical name for the local-number version of
> this is "A2P," but you do not need to know that term to complete it.)

The approval path depends on your number type:

- **Local numbers** go through **SMS approval** (the local business-texting
  registration).
- **Toll-free numbers** use a separate **toll-free verification**, which is
  included in your plan.

See [Toll-free vs local numbers](../phone-numbers/toll-free-vs-local-numbers.md).

## What you can do

- Complete the **SMS Approval Information** section in your account. The details
  you enter are reviewed as part of approval. See
  [What information is needed for SMS approval](what-information-is-needed-for-sms-approval.md).
- Make sure the business details you provide are accurate and match your real
  business — accurate details help approval go smoothly.
- Keep an eye on your **Texting** status to see where you are in the process.

## What to expect

- After you submit, your texting status typically shows **waiting for approval**
  while the review is in progress. This pending state is normal.
- Approval can take time, and we cannot promise an exact timeframe.
- A clinic record that shows the section as **Complete** means your information is
  in; it does not by itself mean texting is live. Texting turns on only after
  approval and configuration finish. See
  [Why SMS is not active immediately](why-sms-is-not-active-immediately.md).

## When to contact support

Contact support if your approval has been pending for an unusually long time, if
you are unsure what a status means, or if you need help understanding what is
being reviewed.
