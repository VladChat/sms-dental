# Landing Page CRO

Use this skill when writing or revising conversion-critical marketing pages.

Core messaging:
- Problem: missed calls become lost patient opportunities.
- Solution: automatic professional SMS follow-up after missed calls.
- Outcome: faster reconnects and better booking recovery.

Page structure:
1. Hero with clear value proposition.
2. Problem statement with practical business impact.
3. How it works in simple steps.
4. Benefits for front desk operations.
5. Compliance and trust section.
6. Pricing clarity.
7. FAQ to remove objections.
8. Final CTA.

CRO rules:
- One primary CTA per section.
- Specific copy over vague marketing terms.
- Remove friction and ambiguity in next steps.
- Keep copy short, direct, and evidence-aware.

Hard constraints:
- No fake social proof or fake urgency.
- No unsupported compliance or medical claims.
- Keep design and tone appropriate for dental offices and payment/review scrutiny.
