# Project Context

Project:
Missed Calls Dental / Dental SMS

Domain:
missedcallsdental.com

Product:
B2B SaaS for dental offices that detects missed calls and sends professional SMS follow-up so offices can reconnect with callers.

Core workflow:
1. Patient calls the office.
2. Office misses the call.
3. System detects the missed call event.
4. Patient receives a short professional SMS.
5. Patient replies.
6. Front desk follows up and books.

Primary outcomes:
- Recover missed-call opportunities.
- Help front desks respond faster.
- Improve trust with clear and compliant communication.

Tone and positioning rules:
- Clear, direct, and professional.
- B2B SaaS style, not consumer hype.
- No fake metrics, testimonials, or urgency.
- No unsupported medical or compliance claims.

Execution rules:
- Prioritize reliability and traceability in workflows.
- Keep UX simple for busy dental staff.
- Ensure copy and product behavior are review-ready for Stripe and Twilio.
- Preserve protected brand assets unless explicitly instructed otherwise.
