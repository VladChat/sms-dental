# Project Context

Project:
Missed Calls Dental / Dental SMS

Domain:
missedcallsdental.com

Product:
B2B SaaS for dental offices that detects missed calls and sends professional SMS follow-up so offices can reconnect with callers.

Target users:
- Dental office owners
- Office managers
- Front desk teams

Core workflow:
1. Patient calls the dental office.
2. Office misses the call.
3. System detects the missed call.
4. Patient receives a short professional SMS from the clinic.
5. Patient replies.
6. Front desk reviews the reply and follows up.

Primary outcomes:
- Recover missed-call opportunities.
- Help front desks respond faster.
- Give missed callers a simple way back to the office.
- Improve trust with clear and professional communication.

Tone and positioning:
- Clear, direct, and professional.
- Simple, trustworthy, and B2B SaaS appropriate.
- Dental-office appropriate, not consumer hype.
- No fake metrics, fake testimonials, or fake urgency.
- No unsupported medical or compliance claims.

Website rules:
- The site must look like a real professional B2B SaaS.
- Use concise human-facing explanations for dental clinic owners.
- Explain the recovery process as missed call → polite SMS → patient reply → front desk follow-up.
- Use real HTML/CSS/SVG for website infographics, not raster images with baked-in text.
- Do not use childish visuals, fake dashboards, fake numbers, or generic AI-looking graphics.

SMS rules:
- Keep SMS short and professional.
- Identify the clinic clearly.
- Support STOP and HELP.
- Do not imply diagnosis, treatment, prescriptions, emergency guidance, or medical advice.
- Do not use spammy wording.

Asset rules:
- Logo, favicon, brand mark, and OG image are protected.
- Do not redraw, distort, or replace protected assets unless Vlad explicitly requests it.
- Marketing graphics are not protected and may be replaced with code-based visuals.

Execution rules:
- Prioritize clarity, reliability, and traceability.
- Keep UX simple for busy dental staff.
- Ensure copy and product behavior are review-ready for Stripe and Twilio.
- Preserve protected brand assets unless explicitly instructed otherwise.
