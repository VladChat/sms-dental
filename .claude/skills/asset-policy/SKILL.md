# Asset Policy

Use this skill when adding, editing, or replacing visual assets.

Protected assets:
- Logo
- Favicon
- Brand mark
- OG image

Rules:
- Do not replace protected assets unless explicitly instructed.
- Marketing graphics may be replaced when beneficial.
- Prefer code-based visuals (HTML/CSS/SVG) over raster text graphics.
- Keep brand presentation professional and B2B-appropriate.

Quality standards:
- Assets must support readable, trustworthy communication.
- Avoid low-quality placeholders and childish motifs.
- Keep visual language consistent with SaaS credibility.

Dental SMS context:
- Graphics should reinforce missed-call recovery workflow and clinic trust.
- Avoid imagery or text that implies medical diagnosis or urgency claims.
