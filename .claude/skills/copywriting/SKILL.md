# Copywriting

Use this skill for headlines, subheads, CTAs, pricing copy, and feature messaging.

Principles:
- Clarity over cleverness.
- Benefits over features.
- Specific language over buzzwords.
- Honest claims only.

Voice:
- Professional, simple, trustworthy, direct.
- Confident but not aggressive.

Do:
- Name the clinic workflow clearly.
- State outcomes in realistic terms.
- Use concise CTA text like:
  - "View pricing"
  - "How it works"
  - "Start setup by email"

Do not:
- Promise diagnosis, treatment, or medical outcomes.
- Use spammy SMS wording or fake urgency.
- Invent stats, logos, or testimonials.

Dental SMS context:
- Emphasize missed-call recovery and practical office follow-up.
- Keep copy aligned with Twilio and Stripe review expectations.
