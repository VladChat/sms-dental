# Dental SMS Project Skill

Project:
Missed Calls Dental / Dental SMS

Domain:
missedcallsdental.com

Product:
B2B SaaS for dental offices that detects missed calls and sends a professional SMS follow-up so the office can reconnect with callers.

Core workflow:
1. Patient calls the dental office.
2. Office misses the call.
3. System detects the missed call.
4. Patient receives a professional SMS.
5. Patient replies.
6. Office follows up.

Tone:
Clear, professional, simple, trustworthy, direct.

Website rules:
- The site must look like a real B2B SaaS.
- No fake statistics.
- No fake testimonials.
- No unsupported compliance claims.
- No medical advice.
- No fake urgency.
- No childish visuals.
- Use real HTML/CSS/SVG for website infographics, not raster images with baked-in text.

SMS rules:
- Keep SMS short and professional.
- Identify the clinic clearly.
- Support STOP and HELP.
- Do not imply diagnosis, treatment, prescriptions, or emergency advice.
- Do not use spammy wording.

Protected assets:
- Logo, favicon, brand mark, and og-image are protected.
- Marketing graphics are not protected and may be replaced with code-based visuals.
