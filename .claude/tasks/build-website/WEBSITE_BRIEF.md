# Website Brief

## Product

Missed Calls Dental / Dental SMS is a B2B SaaS for dental offices.

The product detects missed office calls and sends a professional SMS follow-up so the office can reconnect with callers.

## Domain

```text
missedcallsdental.com
```

## Audience

Primary audience:

- dental office owners
- office managers
- front desk managers
- local clinic decision-makers

They are practical, busy, and skeptical of vague SaaS promises. The website must feel clear, calm, and trustworthy.

## Core problem

Dental offices miss phone calls.

Missed calls can become lost patient opportunities because many callers do not call back.

Front desks are busy. They need a simple way to follow up quickly without adding complicated manual work.

## Core solution

When a dental office misses a call, the system sends a short professional SMS follow-up to the caller.

The caller can reply.

The office reviews the reply and follows up.

## Core workflow

Use this workflow consistently across the website:

1. Patient calls the dental office.
2. Office misses the call.
3. Missed Calls Dental detects the missed call.
4. Patient receives a professional SMS.
5. Patient replies.
6. Front desk follows up.

## Positioning

Primary positioning:

```text
Recover missed patient calls with automatic SMS follow-up.
```

Alternative positioning:

```text
Give missed callers a simple way back to your front desk.
```

## Desired perception

The website must feel:

- professional
- trustworthy
- simple
- B2B SaaS
- dental-office appropriate
- good enough for Stripe review
- good enough for Twilio review
- good enough for real clinic customers

## Avoid

Do not use:

- fake statistics
- fake testimonials
- fake clinic logos
- fake awards
- unsupported compliance claims
- "HIPAA compliant"
- "guaranteed compliant"
- "AI-powered growth platform"
- fake urgency
- medical advice
- diagnosis language
- emergency guidance
- aggressive sales copy
- childish visuals
- emoji as structural icons
- raster infographic images with text baked in

## Business identity

Use:

```text
Missed Calls Dental
support@missedcallsdental.com
```

```text
AllyExporter LLC
```

## Pricing

Use simple pricing language.

Current MVP pricing direction:

```text
$99/month per office
```

Use pricing carefully. Do not invent discounts, trials, or plan tiers unless already present in the repository or explicitly requested by Vlad.

## Call to action

Preferred CTAs:

- View pricing
- How it works
- Start setup by email
- Email support

Avoid unless Vlad explicitly requests otherwise:

- Start free trial
- Book demo
- Get started
- Create account
- Register

## Compliance-sensitive messaging

The site may say:

- STOP and HELP supported.
- Message and data rates may apply.
- Automated texts should not include medical advice.
- Clinic identity is included in SMS follow-up.
- Office staff review patient replies.

Do not overclaim legal or medical compliance.
