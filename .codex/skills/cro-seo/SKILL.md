# CRO SEO

Use this skill for homepage, pricing, landing page, public website, SEO,
conversion copy, trust pages, compliance pages, CTA wording, and review
readiness.

## Positioning

Lead with clear product value:

- Missed Calls Dental helps dental offices recover missed calls with automatic
  SMS follow-up.
- It helps the front desk respond faster to appointment opportunities.
- It works through supported phone-event paths such as forwarding or assigned
  business numbers.

## Copy Rules

- Be clear before clever.
- Use professional, dental-office-appropriate language.
- Explain how the product works without implying it can detect unrelated calls
  automatically.
- Include trust, privacy, opt-out, and support language where relevant.
- Use `example.com` for sample domains or emails.

Avoid:

- Fake statistics.
- Fake testimonials.
- Medical claims or advice.
- Guarantees, scare tactics, or fake urgency.
- Spammy SMS examples.
- Vague AI-platform language that does not match the current MVP.

## Review Readiness

Public pages should be suitable for Stripe and Twilio review:

- Clear business identity.
- Clear product description.
- Clear pricing when pricing is shown.
- Public support contact.
- Privacy, terms, and SMS consent/opt-out language where relevant.
- No unsupported claims about compliance, medical outcomes, or availability.
