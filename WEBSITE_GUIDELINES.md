# Website Guidelines

## Brand and Domain
- Brand name: Missed Calls Dental
- Domain: missedcallsdental.com
- Canonical root URL: https://missedcallsdental.com/
- Support email: support@missedcallsdental.com

## Core Positioning
- Recover missed patient calls with automatic SMS follow-up.

## Audience
- Dental office owners
- Office managers
- Front desk decision makers

## Tone
- Clear and direct
- Short sentences
- Simple words
- Practical business value
- No hype

## Required Navigation
- How it works
- Pricing
- Sign In

## Required Footer Links
- Contact
- Privacy
- Terms
- SMS Consent
- support@missedcallsdental.com

## Homepage Structure
1. Header
2. Hero
3. How it works preview
4. SMS example
5. Benefits
6. Pricing preview
7. Footer

## Required Hero Copy
- Eyebrow: Missed-call SMS recovery for dental offices
- Headline: Recover missed patient calls with automatic SMS follow-up
- Subheadline: When your dental office misses a call, the caller gets a professional text so your team can reconnect.

## Copy Rules
- Keep copy concise.
- Avoid repeated phrasing.
- No fake metrics or testimonials.
- No unsupported compliance claims.
- No fake urgency.

## Compliance-Safe Messaging
- STOP and HELP behavior should be clear where SMS terms are described.
- Avoid medical advice in automated SMS examples.
- Include message-rate language on SMS consent and pricing context where needed.

## Forbidden Phrases
- HIPAA Compliant
- guaranteed compliant
- AI-powered growth platform
- Book Demo
- Start Free Trial
- Get Started
- Create Account
- Register

## Design Direction
- Minimal, calm, and readable
- Mobile-first spacing
- Clean typography
- Clear actions and predictable layout
- No visual clutter
