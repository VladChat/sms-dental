Common questions

A few things people often ask.

Does my main office number change?

No. Your main number stays exactly the same. Missed Calls Dental works alongside it — you keep the line you publish today.

When does billing start?

You aren’t billed during setup or while carrier approval is in progress. Billing starts after your SMS recovery is live, with a 21-day free trial.

What happens if a patient sends STOP?

The opt-out is honored automatically and we won’t text that number again unless they explicitly opt back in.

Is this used for medical advice?

No. The automated texts simply invite the caller to reconnect with your office. Clinical decisions stay with your team.

How fast can we be live?

Most offices finish setup in minutes. Carrier approval timelines depend on the messaging provider; we walk you through everything required.
