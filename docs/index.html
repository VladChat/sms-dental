Missed calls can become missed appointment opportunities

Front desks are busy. When a call is missed, many callers do not call back. Without a fast follow-up, potential appointments are lost.

  • Missed callers often move to the next clinic.
  • Manual callback lists can slow down your team.
  • Inconsistent follow-up creates lost scheduling opportunities.

Simple workflow for missed-call recovery

Missed call -> automatic SMS -> patient reply -> office follow-up -> appointment opportunity.

1

Missed call

A patient calls your office and the call is missed.

2

3

Patient reply

4

Office follow-up

Your front desk reviews replies and follows up.

5

Appointment opportunity

Your team reconnects with more missed callers.

Frequently asked questions

How does missed-call detection work?

Your office routes missed or unanswered calls to the recovery workflow, which then sends an automatic SMS follow-up.

Can this work with our existing phone number?

Yes. The system is designed to support existing office phone workflows.

How do STOP and HELP work?

Recipients can reply STOP to opt out and HELP for support information.

Are message and data rates disclosed?

Yes. SMS program wording includes message and data rate disclosure.

What happens after a patient replies?

Your team receives the reply and can continue follow-up to support scheduling.
