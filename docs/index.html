The problem

Missed calls quietly become lost patients.

Front desks are busy. Callers don’t always try again. Without a quick follow-up, the conversation ends before the office even knows it started.

Busy front desks

Phones ring during checkouts, hand-offs, and patient questions. Calls slip through, even with a great team.

Callers don’t retry

Many new patients call once. If no one answers, they move on to the next office on the list.

Manual follow-up is slow

Pulling missed-call logs and texting back by hand is the kind of task that gets pushed to tomorrow.

How it works

A simple four-step follow-up loop.

No new phone system. No app for the patient. Just a short text after a missed call, and replies your office can act on.

SMS example

A short, professional text the patient actually reads.

Messages clearly identify the clinic, invite a reply, and support opt-out. No medical advice, no scare tactics.

Common patient replies you might see:

  • Yes I’d like an appointment.
  • Call me when you can.
  • Question about insurance or hours.
  • STOP to opt out of further texts.
