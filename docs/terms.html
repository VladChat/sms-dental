Service description

Dental Missed Call Recovery is a missed-call recovery service for dental offices. It may send operational SMS follow-up after a missed, busy, or unanswered call and help route replies to the clinic front desk.

Not a phone replacement

The service is intended as a recovery layer for missed calls. It is not a replacement for a clinic's phone system, front desk, emergency procedures, clinical judgment, or patient communication policies.

No medical advice

The service does not provide diagnosis, treatment advice, prescriptions, emergency response, or clinical decisions. Dental clinics remain responsible for patient communication, scheduling, triage, clinical advice, and compliance obligations.

Emergency notice

The service is not an emergency service. Anyone experiencing a medical emergency should call 911 or seek emergency care immediately.

SMS terms

By participating in the SMS program where permitted, recipients may receive operational messages related to missed-call follow-up, scheduling, callback coordination, and support. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies. Recipients can reply STOP to opt out or HELP for help. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

Clinic responsibilities

  • Respect opt-out requests

  • Follow applicable privacy, healthcare, dental, TCPA, carrier, and messaging rules
  • Test forwarding, SMS delivery, and staff workflow before going live
