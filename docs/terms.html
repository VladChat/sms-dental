Terms

These terms describe the basic use of Missed Calls Dental, operated by AllyExporter LLC.

Service purpose

The service supports missed-call SMS follow-up for dental offices. It is not an emergency service and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment recommendations, or prescription guidance.

Clinic responsibility

Clinics are responsible for their patient communications, phone workflow, and compliance review. Each clinic reviews patient replies and continues the conversation.

Acceptable use

The service is for legitimate dental office follow-up only. It should not be used for unsolicited marketing, fake urgency, medical advice, or any messaging that violates carrier rules or applicable law.

SMS and carriers

SMS delivery depends on carrier networks and recipient settings. Message and data rates may apply for recipients. Carrier rules, including opt-out support, apply at all times.
