Terms

Last updated: May 22, 2026

Missed Calls Dental provides missed-call SMS follow-up for dental offices. The service is a communication layer, not a replacement for office phone systems or clinical decision making.

Service scope

  • Operational SMS after missed or unanswered calls
  • Reply routing so office teams can continue communication
  • Support for STOP and HELP behavior

No medical advice

The service does not provide diagnosis, treatment advice, emergency response, or prescriptions.

Program terms

Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.
