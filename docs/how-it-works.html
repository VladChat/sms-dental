Step by step

From missed call to follow-up, in four steps.

No phone system changes. No app for the patient. A short text bridges the gap so your team can pick up the conversation.

  1. 1

    A patient calls

    A current or prospective patient calls your office.

  2. 2

    The call is missed

    The call goes unanswered, the line is busy, or no one is available.

  3. 3

    A follow-up text is sent

    A short, professional SMS goes out from your clinic identity.

  4. 4

    The office follows up

    Your front desk reviews replies and continues the conversation.

What the office sees

A short summary, then your team takes it from there.

Each reply is captured so the front desk can pick up where the patient left off. Your office stays responsible for scheduling, clinical questions, and follow-through.

If they want an appointment

The front desk follows up to find a time.

If they ask for a call back

The office can call them at the requested time.

If they have a question

The team reviews it and responds with the right context.

If it’s a wrong number or STOP

The office can close it out and the recipient is opted out.

Compliance notes

A few simple rules built into every message.

Clinic identity included

Messages clearly identify the clinic that the patient called.

STOP and HELP supported

Recipients can reply STOP to opt out and HELP for support information.

No medical advice

Automated SMS does not diagnose, recommend treatment, or provide emergency guidance.

Office reviews replies

Your team stays responsible for following up with patients.
