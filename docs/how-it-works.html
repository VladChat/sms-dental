How it works

From missed call to ready-to-book, automatically.

A simple flow that runs in the background. No app to open. No script to write.

1

A call is missed

Unanswered or busy calls to your office are detected. Your main number never changes.

2

The caller gets a text

An automatic, on-brand SMS invites them to reply with what they need.

3

Your team follows up

The front desk sees the caller, their request, and preferred time — ready to book.
