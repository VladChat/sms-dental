How it works

Missed Calls Dental helps your office follow up after missed patient calls with a short, professional SMS.

1. A call is missed

A patient calls your office and the call is missed or unanswered.

2. The caller gets a text

The caller receives a professional SMS asking whether they need help scheduling.

3. Your team follows up

Your office reviews replies and continues the conversation.

SMS example

Hi, this is {{clinic_name}}. We missed your call. Would you like help scheduling an appointment?

Setup notes

  • Works with existing office phone workflows.
  • Patients can reply STOP to opt out.
  • Automated texts should not include medical advice.
