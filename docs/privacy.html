Privacy

Privacy Policy

Missed Calls Dental is operated by AllyExporter LLC. This page describes the basic information used to operate missed-call SMS follow-up for dental offices.

Information we may process

To provide the service, we may process:

  • Business contact details for the clinic and its staff.
  • Clinic account details, including office configuration.
  • Call metadata related to missed, busy, or unanswered calls.
  • SMS metadata, including delivery status.
  • Message content sent or received through the service.
  • Opt-out records, such as STOP responses.
  • Support messages and related correspondence.

How information is used

We use information to send missed-call SMS follow-up, route patient replies to the office, support clinics, maintain opt-out records, and improve service reliability.

Clinic responsibility

Clinics are responsible for their patient communications and for reviewing how the service fits their own privacy and compliance obligations before going live.
