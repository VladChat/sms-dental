Privacy

Last updated: May 22, 2026

This page explains how Missed Calls Dental handles information used to run missed-call SMS follow-up for dental offices.

Information used

We may process business contact details, call metadata, SMS metadata, message content, and opt-out status needed to provide support and run the service.

How information is used

  • Send follow-up SMS after missed office calls
  • Route caller replies to office staff
  • Support STOP and HELP handling
  • Maintain reliability and prevent abuse

SMS consent data

SMS opt-in and consent data is not sold, rented, or shared for third-party marketing.

Contact

Questions can be sent to support@missedcallsdental.com.
