Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how AllyExporter LLC operates the Dental Missed Call Recovery website and related missed-call SMS service.

Information we may process

Depending on how the service is used, we may process business contact information, clinic account information, call metadata, SMS metadata, message content, opt-out status, and basic usage information needed to operate the service.

How information is used

  • To provide missed-call SMS follow-up for participating dental clinics
  • To route replies and callback requests to the clinic front desk
  • To maintain opt-out and HELP handling
  • To monitor service reliability, abuse prevention, and compliance
  • To respond to support or demo requests

SMS consent data

SMS opt-in data and consent will not be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes.

Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be sold, rented, or shared with any third parties.

Service providers

We may use service providers to operate the website, messaging infrastructure, payment processing, hosting, analytics, and support. These providers are used only as needed to operate the service and are not permitted to use SMS consent data for their own marketing.

Dental and health information

The service is designed to keep automated SMS operational and avoid collecting detailed medical history, diagnosis, treatment plans, prescriptions, insurance details, x-rays, or attachments. Participating clinics are responsible for their own legal, HIPAA, privacy, and patient communication obligations.

Opt out

SMS recipients can reply STOP to opt out. Recipients can reply HELP for support information.

Data retention

We retain information only as needed to provide the service, maintain records, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, prevent abuse, and improve the product.

For privacy questions, contact office@allyexporter.com.
