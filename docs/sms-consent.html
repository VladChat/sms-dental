SMS Consent

SMS consent and opt-out

Missed Calls Dental sends operational SMS follow-up after a missed dental office call.

Why someone may receive a text

A caller may receive an SMS when they call a participating dental office and the call is missed, busy, or unanswered.

Opt-out and support

Recipients can reply STOP to opt out and HELP for support information. Message and data rates may apply.

No medical advice

Automated SMS messages should not include diagnosis, treatment advice, prescriptions, or emergency guidance.
