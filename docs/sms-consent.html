SMS consent

Missed Calls Dental sends short, operational SMS follow-up after a missed dental office call.

Why someone may receive a text

A caller may receive an SMS when they call a participating dental office and the call is missed, busy, or unanswered. The message identifies the clinic and invites a reply.

Opt-out and support

Reply STOP to opt out of future automated messages, or reply HELP for support information. Message and data rates may apply.

No medical advice

Automated SMS messages do not include diagnosis, treatment recommendations, prescriptions, or emergency guidance. For clinical questions, contact your clinic directly. For emergencies, call emergency services.

Office review

Replies are reviewed by the office. Your clinic stays responsible for scheduling, clinical decisions, and continuing the conversation.

Contact

For SMS or opt-out questions, email support@missedcallsdental.com.
