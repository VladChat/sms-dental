# Page CRO Dental SaaS Skill

Use this skill for landing page, pricing page, homepage, website copy, trust sections, and conversion work for the Dental SMS project.

Project:
Dental SMS

Domain:
dentalmissedcall.com

Product:
A B2B SaaS for dental clinics that detects missed calls and automatically sends a professional SMS follow-up to help recover missed patients and book appointments.

Use this skill when the task involves:
- Homepage
- Landing page
- Pricing page
- CTA buttons
- Hero section
- Trust sections
- Feature sections
- FAQ
- Conversion rate optimization
- Stripe verification readiness
- Twilio review readiness
- SaaS positioning
- Dental clinic marketing copy
- Website clarity
- Website credibility

Main positioning:
Dental SMS helps dental clinics recover missed calls with automatic SMS follow-up.

Core value:
- Dental offices miss calls.
- Missed calls become lost patients.
- Fast SMS follow-up can help bring patients back.
- The product helps front desks respond faster without extra manual work.

Target customer:
- Dental clinics
- Dental offices
- Local healthcare offices with phone-heavy front desks

Main headline direction:
"Recover missed dental calls with automatic SMS follow-up"

Alternative headline direction:
"Turn missed calls into booked dental appointments"

Simple subheadline:
"Dental SMS automatically texts patients after a missed call, so your office can respond faster and recover more appointment opportunities."

CTA examples:
- Start free trial
- See how it works
- Get started
- Book a demo

Avoid:
- Fake statistics
- Fake testimonials
- Medical promises
- Overhyped claims
- Enterprise buzzwords
- Childish startup design
- Placeholder text
- Vague claims like "AI-powered growth platform"
- Spammy SMS examples

Website must look:
- Professional
- Simple
- Trustworthy
- B2B SaaS
- Clean
- Dental-office appropriate
- Good enough for Stripe verification
- Good enough for Twilio review
- Good enough for real clinic customers

Recommended page sections:
1. Hero
2. Problem: missed calls cost clinics patients
3. How it works
4. Product benefits
5. SMS example
6. Trust/compliance section
7. Pricing
8. FAQ
9. Final CTA

Trust points:
- Clear business identity
- Clear product explanation
- Clear pricing
- Clear contact/support information
- No fake claims
- No misleading medical language
- Professional SMS compliance language
- Privacy and opt-out awareness

Pricing copy style:
Keep pricing simple and clear. Avoid confusing tiers unless needed.

Suggested pricing framing:
"Simple monthly pricing for dental offices that want to recover missed calls."

FAQ topics:
- How does it detect missed calls?
- Does it work with our existing phone number?
- Can patients opt out?
- Is this compliant?
- What happens after a patient replies?
- Can we cancel anytime?

Tone:
Clear.
Direct.
Professional.
Simple.
No hype.
No fluff.

For this project, page/CRO work must prioritize:
1. Clarity
2. Trust
3. Conversion
4. Stripe verification readiness
5. Twilio review readiness
6. Dental clinic credibility
