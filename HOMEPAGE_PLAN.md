# Homepage Plan

## Page Goal
Present a short, clear B2B SaaS homepage for dental offices.

## Required Sections
1. Header
2. Hero
3. How it works preview
4. SMS example
5. Benefits
6. Pricing preview
7. Footer

## Header
- Brand: Missed Calls Dental
- Navigation: How it works | Pricing | Sign In

## Hero
- Eyebrow: Missed-call SMS recovery for dental offices
- Headline: Recover missed patient calls with automatic SMS follow-up
- Subheadline: When your dental office misses a call, the caller gets a professional text so your team can reconnect.
- Primary action: View Pricing
- Secondary action: How it works

## How It Works Preview (3 steps)
1. A call is missed
2. The caller gets a text
3. Your team follows up

## SMS Example
- Hi, this is {{clinic_name}}. We missed your call. Would you like help scheduling an appointment?
- Microcopy: STOP and HELP supported.

## Benefits (3 cards)
- Respond faster: Send a prompt follow-up after a missed call.
- Recover more callers: Give missed callers an easy way back to your office.
- Reduce manual follow-up: Help the front desk focus on replies that need attention.
