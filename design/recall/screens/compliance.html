About this profile

Bright Smile Dental uses Missed Calls Dental to follow up by text when we’re unable to answer a patient call. This profile describes who we are and how we message.

When we text you

You may receive a text from us only after you call our office and we are unable to answer. The message invites you to share how we can help so our front desk can follow up.

  • Messages are sent only in response to a missed call you placed to us.
  • We do not send marketing or promotional texts.
  • Message frequency is limited to your active conversation with our office.

Your consent and choices

By calling our office, you may receive a one-time follow-up text. You can reply STOP at any time to opt out, or HELP for assistance. Message and data rates may apply.

Standard rates may apply.Your mobile carrier’s message and data rates may apply to texts you exchange with us.

Information we handle

When you text our office, we process your phone number and the contents of your messages solely to follow up about your inquiry and to help schedule care.

How we use it

  • To respond to your missed call and assist with scheduling.
  • To maintain a record of your conversation with our front desk.
  • To meet legal and carrier compliance obligations.

What we don’t do

We do not sell your information. We do not use your phone number for marketing. Message content is not used for advertising.

Data retention & requests

We retain messages only as long as needed to assist you and meet our obligations. To request deletion of your information, contact our office at (224) 555-1234.

Last updated May 2026

SMS program terms

By calling Bright Smile Dental, you consent to receive a follow-up text message if we are unable to answer. This is a transactional message, not marketing.

Opting out

Reply STOP to any message to stop receiving texts. You’ll receive one confirmation, and we won’t text you again unless you contact us first.

Getting help

Reply HELP for assistance, or call our office at (224) 555-1234. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies based on your conversation.

Supported carriers

Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages. Service availability depends on your mobile carrier.
