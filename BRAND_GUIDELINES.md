# Brand Guidelines

## Brand Name
- Missed Calls Dental

## Product Position
- Missed-call SMS recovery for dental offices.
- Core promise: automatic SMS follow-up helps office teams reconnect with callers.

## Domain and Support
- Site domain: missedcallsdental.com
- Canonical root: https://missedcallsdental.com/
- Support email: support@missedcallsdental.com

## Logo Rules
- Use approved raster logo mark only.
- Logo asset: `brand/logo-mark.webp` (docs) and matching `public/brand/logo-mark.webp`.
- Do not redraw or regenerate logo artwork.

## Visual Direction
- Minimal and professional.
- High readability.
- Calm palette.
- Consistent spacing.

## UX Direction
- Keep sections short.
- Make actions obvious.
- Prioritize scanability on mobile.
- Avoid dense layouts.

## Navigation Standard
- How it works | Pricing | Sign In

## Footer Standard
- Missed Calls Dental
- Automatic SMS follow-up for missed patient calls.
- Contact, Privacy, Terms, SMS Consent
- support@missedcallsdental.com

## Messaging Rules
- Short, specific statements.
- No fake statistics.
- No fake testimonials.
- No unsupported compliance claims.
- No medical advice language in automated SMS examples.
